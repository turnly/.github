### What's happening?

We are building the next management system for queues and walk-ins at physical locations.

Don't think of it in the traditional, boring way we've seen so far, we think of the
capabilities and superpowers your organization can gain from improving the
customer experience by eliminating queues in our modern age.

Smart devices are everywhere and the capabilities are endless,
we plan to use this advantage to take queuing systems to the next level.
